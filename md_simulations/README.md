## Simulation Parameters

- **Force field:** Amber14 (amber14-all.xml, amber14/tip3p.xml)
- **Water model:** TIP3P explicit solvent
- **Padding:** 0.8 nm from protein
- **Temperature:** 310 K (Langevin thermostat)
- **Timestep:** 2 fs
- **Constraints:** H-bond constraints (SHAKE)
- **Electrostatics:** PME (1.0 nm cutoff)
- **Minimization:** 1000 steps steepest descent
- **Equilibration:** 10 ps NVT
